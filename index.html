MetaPhoto

Explore, learn, and share your favorite Rock Climbing, anywhere in the world!

MetaPhoto is a one stop shop for the rock climbing enthusiast. Explore routes and climbs near you, or search for locations anywhere in the world! Create an account and you can upload photos of your own adventures, check out other user's photos, create favorite lists for future exploration, and even see detailed information on any routes available. Built using the MERN stack on a team of three people.

About Quinn

Currently Located: Sacramento, Ca

Favorites: React, Semantic Ui, and simple, elegant, functional design
I started my career in web development in the summer of 2020, when I decided one day that I was going to teach myself C++. Turns out that is not the fastest, or most effective way to get yourself coding full time. After a few months of learning, I took the plunge and enrolled in the Full-Stack Web Developer boot camp. Completing that boot camp gave me the skills, the mindset, and a relentless drive to keep learning and coding. I hope to find a place for myself where I get to create products and services that aren't just helpful, but are also beautiful, intuitive, fun to use technological feats, that keep people coming back for more time and time again.
